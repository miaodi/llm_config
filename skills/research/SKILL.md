# Research Skill

## Purpose
Support investigation, source comparison, and structured synthesis.

## When To Use
Use for competitive analysis, technical evaluation, or literature review.

## Workflow
1. Clarify the research question and constraints.
2. Gather primary sources first when possible.
3. Summarize findings, uncertainties, and recommended next actions.

## Output
Concise synthesis with sources and open questions.
